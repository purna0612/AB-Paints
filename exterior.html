Exterior Emulsion

Emulsion paint is water-based paint, which contains small polymer particles that have pigments inside. ... After the paint dries, the particles combine, producing a film of paint on the wall. Emulsion paint can be used for the exterior as well as the interior of the house. The water based paint will come off eventually with rain and normal activity on the paving slabs, but it will take a while. The only guaranteed way is to use water and a scrubbing brush or, if you don't want to get down on your hands and knees, a stiff bristled yard brush.
