Organization

Firmly rooted in the kupondole, Lalitpur, since 2006, AB Paints and Chemical Industries Pvt. Ltd. began as a small, family-owned business that, through hard work and commitment to quality, is planning to become one of the country's leading independent manufacturers and distributors of paints and painting supplies.
AB Paints & Chemical Industries Pvt. Ltd, itself as a quality paint manufacturer that has an incredibly intense focus on quality control. Our factory facility is equipped with laboratories and all the necessary equipment and machinery required to manufacture excellent quality paint coatings. We manufacture according to both NS(Nepal Standard) as well as ISO 9001:2015 specifications. AB Paints & Chemical Industries Pvt. Ltd has invested in only the best laboratory equipment to guarantee that its end users enjoy a lasting coating on their projects.
